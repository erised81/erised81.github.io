⚔ The Trials
🔬
Trial 1
The Specimen Preparator
After a visit, describe one fossil you worked on: where it was excavated, what layer of rock it came from, and what tools you used to clean it. Sketch or photograph it if you can.
🦎
Trial 2
The Keeper of Scales
After a reptile care session, record what you observed: what the animal ate, how it behaved, what its habitat needs are, and one thing that surprised you about caring for a living reptile vs. studying an ancient one.
⚔ Main Challenge
🏆 The Deep Time Dispatch
After your 5th visit, write or record a "Field Report" — like a real paleontologist would send back from a dig site. Include: (1) what specimens you worked on across your visits, (2) one thing you know now that you didn't know before you started, and (3) what question you most want to answer next. Any format works: written, voice memo, video, slides, or drawing.
