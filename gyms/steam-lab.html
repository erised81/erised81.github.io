Main Challenge
Deep Dive
🔒
💬 Volcanion's Question If an exothermic chemical reaction occurs, the surroundings become warmer. Where does that energy come from? How does this connect to what happened in Trial 1?
📦 Deep Dive — included with Activity 2 in your box
✍ Written response 🎙 Spoken aloud 📝 Voice memo 💻 Typed
Time estimate: 15–20 minutes.

This is the Deep Dive prompt included as an optional extension in Activity 2. It's been elevated here as the Main Challenge because it's where the real thinking happens — Cole connects the hands-on experiment to the conceptual explanation from the video.

What a good response looks like: Any answer that names where the energy comes from (chemical bonds breaking and reforming) and links it back to something observable from Trial 1 — even one sentence of connection is the skill.

Output is flexible — spoken, typed, written, or talked through with you. First draft is the draft; no revision needed unless Cole wants to.

Wisconsin standards: NGSS MS-PS3-4 — Energy transfer; W.8.2 — Informational/explanatory writing.
